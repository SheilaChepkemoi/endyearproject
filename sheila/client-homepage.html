About Us

Welcome to Jowath Events Solution online portal. Jowath Event Solutions Limited is an event rental organization that is located in Kahawa Sukari; Nairobi, Kenya. The organization was started in August 2017, operating from their home residence. Over the years the business grew and they were able to settle in Kahawa Sukari. The organization offers rental services for weddings, corporate, parties among other events. designed to help you organize your events. We hire out a wide range of products and services. We offer tent solutions;making and hiring,catering solutions;hiring glassware,table accessories, cutlery and crockery,furniture solutions;tables,chairs,decorative linen and drapping materials.
